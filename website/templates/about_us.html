Frequently Asked Questions

Why do we ask about mental health?
- Mental health is an indicator of over exercise, and can be deteramental to and athletes performance. We belive that mental health come before anything else
How often should I use the website?
- We prefer filling out the form daily, but using it every time you exercise works too
How does this prevent injury?
- Over exercising is one of the leading causes of injury, we believe that if you take the appropriate time to rest that you will be able to compete at your highest capability and prevent injury
